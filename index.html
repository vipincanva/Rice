Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

A delicious Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Spaghetti Carbonara is a simple yet flavorful Italian dish that has captured hearts all over the world. It combines creamy egg yolks, salty pancetta, and sharp Parmesan cheese for a comforting and satisfying meal. Best served with freshly cracked black pepper and a side of garlic bread!

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti according to the package directions.
  2. Meanwhile, cook the pancetta in a pan over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and Parmesan cheese until smooth.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and reserve some pasta water.
  5. Add the hot pasta to the pan with pancetta, then pour in the egg and cheese mixture. Toss quickly to create a creamy sauce, adding pasta water if necessary to reach the right consistency.
  6. Season with freshly ground black pepper and serve immediately with more Parmesan cheese on top.
